Chicken

Description

This is a tasty dish made using chicken and lots of Indian spices and masalas.

Ingridients

  • 1 kg Chicken
  • Onions
  • Mustard oil
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Cloves
  • Green and Red chilles
  • Curry leaves
  • Peppercorns
  • Garam masala and other spices

Steps:

  1. Massage chicken with a blend of spices and curd and leave it to marinate.
  2. Put bay leaf, curry leaf, green chilli, jeera in hot mustard oil.
  3. Add onions into the pan and cook until they are golden brown.
  4. After that add salt to taste and turmeric to the cooking pan.
  5. Add masala to the pan after some time and then add chicken to the pan and fry.
  6. Fry for some time and then add red chilly powder and cook till chicken is cooked through.
  7. Add water to the pan and let the dish simmer for some time.
  8. Reduce the gravy for some time and then add coriander for garnish.
